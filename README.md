# CECS-491-Budget-Tracker-Project
This is a repository for our Budget Tracker project.

## Problem Statement

With the price of groceries, rent and other necessies on the rise, the higher usage rates of credit/debt cards, and unexpected expenses cause many ordinary people have trouble budgeting their money for all their needs. 


For this reason we seek to provide a Budget Tracker applications to help people create and maintain monthly budgets.

## Learn About Budgeting

In this section we will provide some information about budgeting, making saving goals and sticking to a budget.

Budgeting is the act of creating an outline for how to spend money over a specified time period. 
Budgeting is an important part of adult life. Unfortunately budgeting can be difficult for people for various reasons.
